Healthy Eating Basics

Introduction

Eating a balanced diet is essential for good health. It provides the body with energy, supports growth, and helps prevent diseases. Remember: food is fuel for your body!

Special Characters

Steps to Start Eating Healthy

  1. Plan your meals
  2. Include fruits and vegetables
  3. Choose whole grains
  4. Limit processed foods
  5. Drink plenty of water

Examples of Healthy Foods

  • Apples
  • Spinach
  • Brown rice
  • Almonds
  • Salmon

Nutrition Terms

Calories
A measure of energy provided by food.
Protein
Helps build and repair tissues.
Fiber
Aids digestion and keeps you full longer.
